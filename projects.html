War and Pieces

(Current)

War and Pieces will be a roguelite, with a combat-system based on the classic card game, War.
Battle through a map of 2 encounters before reaching the boss.
Beat 7 of these Bosses to defeat the Super Boss and win the Run!
Don't worry if you lose, though.
You gain XP from each run that grants you upgrades for future runs!

War and Pieces is currently in development! Please check back later for any updates!
